# Privacy Policy for Blindboxd

**Last Updated: March 4, 2026**

Your privacy is a top priority for **Blindboxd**. This extension is designed to be functional, lightweight, and completely private.

## 1. Data Collection

**Blindboxd does not collect any personal information.**

* We do not collect your name, email address, Letterboxd credentials, or browsing history.
* We do not use any tracking tools (like Google Analytics) or third-party cookies.

## 2. Data Storage

To remember which movies you have chosen to reveal, the extension stores specific Movie IDs.

* **Local Storage Only:** These IDs are stored exclusively on your device using your browser's internal storage (`localStorage` and `chrome.storage.local`).
* **No Cloud Sync:** This data is not synchronized across your devices unless you are using a browser-level sync feature provided by Google or Mozilla.

## 3. Third-Party Transmission

**Your data never leaves your computer.**

* No movie IDs, settings, or preferences are ever transmitted to an external server.
* The extension does not communicate with any third-party APIs or databases.

## 4. User Control & Deletion

You have full control over the small amount of data the extension stores:

* **Manual Reset:** You can clear all stored movie IDs at any time by clicking the "Reset Memory" button in the extension popup.
* **Browser Settings:** Clearing your browser's cache or local storage will also remove all data associated with Blindboxd.
* **Uninstallation:** If you uninstall the extension, all associated settings and data are automatically deleted by the browser.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

If this extension is updated to include new features that require data handling, this policy will be updated accordingly. However, the core principle of **"Local First, Privacy Always"** will remain.

## 6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the code, you can open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://www.google.com/search?q=YOUR_GITHUB_REPO_LINK_HERE).
